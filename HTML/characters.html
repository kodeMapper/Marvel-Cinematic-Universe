Heroes

Captain America

Super-soldier with a vibranium shield, leading the Avengers with unwavering bravery and justice.

Iron Man

Genius billionaire in a high-tech suit of armor, known for his innovation and heroic tech prowess.

Hulk

Scientist with incredible strength and green skin, transforming into a powerful giant when enraged.

Spiderman

Teen superhero with spider-like abilities and a web-slinging suit, balancing heroics and high school life.

Thor

Norse god of thunder wielding the enchanted hammer Mjölnir, protector of Asgard and Earth.

Villains

Thanos

Mad Titan with immense power, seeking to balance the universe with the Infinity Stones.

Abonimation

Hulk’s monstrous adversary, possessing similar powers but driven by malevolence.

Ebony

Charismatic and manipulative alien, loyal to Thanos, with telekinetic powers and a knack for persuasion.

Doctor Doom

Genius inventor and sorcerer with a metal suit and a relentless quest for global domination.

kang the Conquerer

Time-traveling warlord with advanced technology, known for his complex and formidable history.

Other Characters

Nick Fury

Director of S.H.I.E.L.D., orchestrating global defense with strategic genius and a network of heroes.

Aunt May

Spider-Man's beloved aunt, offering wisdom and support while unaware of his superhero identity.

Ancient One

The Sorcerer Supreme who protected Earth from mystical threats with profound knowledge and magical abilities.

Jane Foster

Astrophysicist who becomes Thor's love interest and occasionally wields Mjölnir herself.

Odin

All-Father of Asgard, ruler of the gods, and Thor’s father with vast power and wisdom.

Valkyrie

Fierce Asgardian warrior, skilled in combat and leader of the Valkyrior, known for her strength and honor.

Heimdall

Guardian of the Bifrost with all-seeing vision, protecting the realm of Asgard and its connections.

Shuri

Brilliant scientist and princess of Wakanda, known for her technological innovations and leadership.
